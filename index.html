🧠 Privacy Policy for FocusBrain

Last Updated: April 2026

Introduction

FocusBrain ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application FocusBrain (the "App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  1. Display Name
    • Optional display name (stored locally on your device)
  2. Task Data
    • Tasks you create
    • Energy check-in responses
    • Timer session data
    • Completed tasks and achievements
  3. Usage Preferences
    • App settings
    • Notification preferences
    • Sound preferences

Information Collected Automatically

  1. Device Information
    • Device type and model
    • Operating system version
    • Anonymous device identifier (generated randomly, not linked to any personal account)
    • App version
  2. Usage Data
    • Features used
    • Time spent in app
    • Crash reports and performance data
  3. Analytics Data
    • We use Firebase Analytics to understand app usage
    • This includes anonymized usage patterns

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  1. Provide and Maintain the App - Enable core functionality, sync your data across devices, process your tasks and track progress
  2. Improve the App - Analyze usage patterns, fix bugs and improve performance, develop new features
  3. Personalize Your Experience - Match tasks to your energy levels, provide relevant recommendations, customize notifications
  4. AI Features - When you use AI features (task breakdown, daily planner, smart suggestions, weekly report), your task titles and energy levels are sent to OpenAI through our secure servers. We do not store the AI-generated content on our servers.
  5. Subscription Management - Process and manage your subscription through RevenueCat, verify your premium status

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • Most of your data is stored locally on your device
  • This includes tasks, settings, and preferences

Cloud Storage

  • An anonymous device identifier is stored in Firebase Firestore for rate limiting, notifications, and premium status verification
  • Premium users can back up and restore their data via Firebase Cloud Firestore
  • No email address or personal account is required

Security Measures

  • We use industry-standard encryption
  • Data transmitted to our servers uses HTTPS
  • We regularly review our security practices

RevenueCat Privacy Policy

Data Retention

  • Local data: Retained until you delete the app or clear app data
  • Cloud data: Anonymous device identifier and usage data are retained until you uninstall the app or request deletion
  • Analytics data: Retained for up to 14 months

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  1. Access Your Data - View all data stored in the app, request a copy of your data
  2. Delete Your Data - Delete individual tasks, clear all local data by uninstalling the app, request deletion of cloud data by contacting us
  3. Opt-Out - Disable analytics collection, turn off notifications, manage ad personalization through device settings

Children's Privacy

FocusBrain is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. These countries may have different data protection laws. By using the App, you consent to the transfer of information to countries outside your country of residence.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app, updating the "Last Updated" date, and sending a notification for significant changes.
